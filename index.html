Hello

Hey, hello, my name is Sarah Bootsma. I'm a designer & illustrator currently residing in Ottawa, Canada. I'm a keen learner with a love for many different forms of design, but I have a particular passion for illustration and branding. In all of my works, I strive to create authentic, creative, and timeless pieces–paying close attention to detail. When I'm not designing, you can catch me travelling, spending time with family, or curled up with a cup of tea watching netflix.

Work

PillPal iOS App

Pillpal is an iOS app I designed in order to prevent individuals from forgetting to take their prescribed medication, the right way, at the right time. I took a minimalistic approach to the design of this app's interface, with the use of flat design & simple icons.

Grand Budapest Hotel Poster

This is a poster I designed for the movie The Grand Budapest Hotel. I incorporated some key imagery from the film into the poster–keeping it simple with a pink and blue colour scheme which allowed the main elements to pop out from the muted background.

Manhattan's Burgers Rebrand

This was a self-initiated project I created for Manhattan's Handmade Burgers, located around Ontario, Canada. I decided to take a hands on approach, by creating a handmade logotype with the use of a brush pen. I felt that this rebrand gave the company a fresher, more current look and suited the company more efficiently, to give it that handmade feel.

Bates Motel Icon Poster

This is a poster I created for the television show Bates Motel, which is based off the old film Psycho. The poster features a variety of icons which represent different important parts from the first season of the show. The colours chosen are based off the Bates Motel signage seen throughout the seasons.

The Piggy Market Rebrand

The Piggy Market is a butchery located in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. They offer a variety of locally grown & raised products such as: butcher cuts, deli meats, cheeses and pantry stapes, prepared foods & locally grown product in the main growing season. My goal for this rebrand was to create a more current, time-less logo design, which would communicate the purpose of the business more effectively than the existing.

Let's Get Lost Coffee Table Book

This is a travel guide coffee table book I designed. I created simple iconic illustrations to compliment each different state/city, with an easy-to-follow layout.

Wander Broadcast Package

This is a broadcast package I created for a fictional tv channel called Wander. Wander is a reality travel channel, which features backpackers recorded stories and adventures. The package contains an opening title, bumper, transition, and a lower thirds.
