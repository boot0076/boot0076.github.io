PillPal iOS App

Pillpal is an iOS app designed to prevent individuals from forgetting to take their prescribed medication, the right way, at the right time. Pillpal allows users to scan their prescription bottle, and all the necessary information regarding that medication will be stored into the app. The user will then be given dose notifications when it is time to take the medication, along with important information to keep in mind when taking their dose.

Bates Motel Icon Poster

This is a poster I created for the television show Bates Motel, which is based off the old film Psycho. The poster features a variety of icons which represent various important parts from the first season of the show. The colours chosen are based off the Bates Motel signage and colour scheme seen throughout the season.

Manhattan's Burgers Rebrand

This is a rebranding concept for a local burger joint in Ontario, Canada. Manhattan's Handmade Burgers focuses on providing their customers with 100% beef, no filler, juicy handmade burgers. With this, I decided to take a hands on approach by creating a handmade logotype with the use of a brush pen. I felt that this rebrand gave the company a fresher, more current look and suited the company more efficiently–giving it that handmade feel.

Grand Budapest Hotel Poster

This is a movie poster I designed for the film, The Grand Budapest Hotel. My goal was to incorporate the most memorable elements from the movie together within a single layout. With this, I illustrated a scene which ties all the important parts together with the use of a high contrasted pink, and muted background.

The Piggy Market Rebrand

The Piggy Market is a butchery located in Ottawa, Canada. They offer a variety of locally grown & raised products such as: butcher cuts, deli meats, cheeses and pantry stapes, prepared foods & locally grown product in the main growing season. My goal for this rebrand was to create a more current, timeless logo design, which would communicate the purpose of the business more effectively than the existing.

Back to the Future

This is a soundtrack album cover I designed for the classic film, Back to the Future. My concept was to include the Delorean Time Machine car and clock building together into a simplified composition. I felt these visuals were the most memorable from the film, and would allow for quick identification.

Fetch iOS App

Fetch is an iOS app designed to keep pet lover's in touch with their furry friends at all times. It works with the use of a GPS tracker that connects to their collar tag. Whether you've lost them at the park, or they're hiding somewhere around your neighbourhood, Fetch will ensure you never lose sight of your pet again.
